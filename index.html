My Story

"From Trinidad & Tobago to Microsoft — built on curiosity, driven by advocacy."

My journey began with a deep love of mathematics and systems thinking — earning actuarial science credentials (SOA Exam P & FM) before discovering my true calling: helping others unlock their potential through learning.

With over 5 years at Microsoft, I serve as a Data & AI Senior Technical Trainer, delivering transformative learning experiences that bridge technical complexity with human understanding.

I am a certified Project Management Professional (PMP®), a Microsoft Certified Trainer (MCT), and hold a Green Project Management (GPM-b) certification — bringing technical depth, strategic thinking, and sustainable practice.
